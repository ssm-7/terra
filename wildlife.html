Ecosystem Profiles

Archiving real-time population structural metadata and tracking critical migratory behaviors across vulnerable world biomes.

Critically Endangered

Amur Leopard

With fewer than 100 individuals remaining in the wild, these highly adapted, solitary predators survive along the mountainous temperate forest border regions of the Far East.

Vulnerable

African Elephant

Vital keystones of their native biomes, engineering entire savannah landscapes. Heavy fragmentation of historical territory continues to present severe structural challenges.

Endangered

Green Sea Turtle

Navigating complex marine currents for thousands of miles, these ancient ocean wanderers rely directly on the micro-stability of coastal reef architectures and protected nesting beaches.
